Module 3 Progress Report

I have officially started studying the CompTIA Security+ material and have completed the first three lessons:

  • Lesson 1: Security Fundamentals – Covered essential concepts such as the CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability), basic security controls, and the importance of layered security (defense in depth).
  • Lesson 2: Threat Management and Cybersecurity – Introduced the various types of cybersecurity threats, including insider threats, malware, phishing, and social engineering. Also discussed strategies for identifying, mitigating, and responding to threats.
  • Lesson 3: Threats and Attacks on Endpoints – Focused on endpoint vulnerabilities and how devices such as laptops, mobile phones, and IoT devices can be targeted. Explored mitigation strategies like patching, endpoint protection platforms (EPP), and strong authentication methods.
